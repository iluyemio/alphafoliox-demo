About AlphaFolio X

We are a P2P digital currency exchange.

AlphaFolio X is a platform where individuals can trade and manage cryptocurrencies, and digital assets conveniently. We offer a trusted platform for cryptocurrency transactions.

Our mission is to leverage use of the blockchain and other frontier technologies to solve problems in society.

At AlphaFolio X, we have a vision: to be the infrastructure for blockchain payments across emerging markets.

Passion
